# How to create a custom fare?

In order to configure fares, it's essential to adhere to the government-regulated fares in each country and city. Additionally, you have the flexibility to establish fares based on your own discretion, ensuring fairness for both yourself and your clients, taking into account factors such as the day and time.

**How to customize your fares?**

Follow these steps:

* Tap "Rides" and select "Custom."
* Click on "Manage Custom Fare."
* Press the + sign in the top right corner.
* Assign a name to your custom Fare.
* Add the details of your Fare.
