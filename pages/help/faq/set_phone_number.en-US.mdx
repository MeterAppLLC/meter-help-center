# How to set up your phone number?

Your phone number will be displayed for invoices and receipts.

Follow these steps:

* Log in to your Meter account through the website.
* [Click here to log in](https://www.meterapp.co/ar/signin).
* Once inside your account profile, scroll down the page until you find the option "Phone Number".
* Tap on the option "Update Phone Number", add your phone number, then tap "Submit Phone". We will send a one-time verification code to your mobile number that you must enter to verify your number.
* After you enter the one-time verification code, tap "Submit Phone".
