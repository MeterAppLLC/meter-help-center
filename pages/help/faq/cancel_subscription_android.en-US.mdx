# How do I cancel my Meter App subscription purchased through the Play Store?

 In order to cancel your Meter App subscription, follow these steps.

* Go on your Android device and make sure to sign in to the Google Account that has your subscriptions.
* Tap here to go to and [subscriptions in Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions). 
* Select the subscription you want to cancel.
* Tap Cancel subscription.
