# How to Cancel Your Meter Subscription on Android

If you subscribed to Meter through the Google Play Store on your Android device and need to cancel your subscription, follow these steps:

## Step 1: Visit the Google Play Help Center

1. **Go to the Official Google Play Instructions**:
- Navigate to the [Google Play Help Center](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/7018481).

## Step 2: Follow Google’s Official Instructions

1. **Read the Instructions**:
- Follow the official Google instructions provided on the page to **Cancel, Pause, or Change a Subscription** on Google Play.

> **Note:** Cancelling your subscription will take effect at the end of your current billing period as per Google Play’s policies. Ensure you follow the steps accurately to manage your subscription.

If you have any questions or encounter issues, please contact our [support team](mailto:support@meterapp.co).
