# Are you having trouble receiving email receipts?

If you can't find the receipt, check your spam folder in your email. Alternatively, you can use Stripe QR code payments, which generate receipts and will be automatically sent to the email the rider chooses after paying.
Learn more about Stripe QR code payments by [clicking this link](https://help.meterapp.co/help/app-features/stripe-qr-code-payments).
