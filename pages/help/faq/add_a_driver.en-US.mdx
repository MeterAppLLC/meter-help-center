# How to Update the Quantity of Your Meter Fleet Plan

If you need to add more drivers to your fleet, you can update the quantity of your Meter fleet plan. Follow these steps to adjust your plan:

## Step 1: Access Your Subscription Management

1. **Visit the Account Management Page**:
- Navigate to [Manage Subscription](https://www.meterapp.co/account/manage_subscription).

2. **Open Customer Portal**:
- Click on the **Open Customer Portal** button.

## Step 2: Update Your Plan

1. **Click on Update Plan**:
- In the customer portal, click on the **Update Plan** button.

2. **Change the Quantity of Drivers**:
- Adjust the **quantity of drivers** to the desired new amount.

3. **Confirm the New Quantity**:
- Click **Continue** and confirm the new amount.

> **Note:** Make sure to review the changes before confirming as they will affect your billing and fleet capacity.

If you have any questions or encounter issues, please contact our [support team](mailto:support@meterapp.co).
