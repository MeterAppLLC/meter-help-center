# How do I add a driver to my Meter enterprise fleet plan?

In order  to add one or more drivers to your current  Meter enterprise fleet plan
follow these steps.

* Log in to your Meter account through the website.
* [Click here to log in](https://www.meterapp.co/ar/signin).
* Once inside your account profile,  Tap on "Fleet" > "Fleet Pricing".
* Select Meter enterprise fleet > “Manage” 
* Tap on “Customer portal”, > click  on “Updated plan”.
* Add the Quantity of drivers  that you want in your plan. 
* To finalize the process, tap “Continue”  and confirm.
