# How to Change the Language of Meter App?

**How to Change the Language Settings for Meter App on Your Android Phone?**

You can change the language of an individual app in the device settings.

* On your device, open the Settings app.
* Tap "System", then "Languages & input", followed by "App languages".
* Select the app you want to change.
* Choose a language.

**How to Change the Language on iPhone?**

* Open the Settings app.
* Tap "General".
* Select "Language & Region".
* Tap "Add Language".
* Select the language.
* Set your primary language.

**Note:** An alert will appear asking which language you want to use as your primary language. Once the language is updated, your device will automatically display the selected language.
