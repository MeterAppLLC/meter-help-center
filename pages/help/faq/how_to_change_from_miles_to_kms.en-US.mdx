# How do I switch from mi/km or km/mi?

Follow these steps:

* Log in to your Meter account.
* Select "Trips."
* Select "Manage custom rates."
* [Select this link if you want to learn how to create custom rates](https://help.meterapp.co/es-ES/help/pro-features/custom_fares).
* Choose an existing rate or create a new one.
* Within your rate, scroll down and select:
   - On Android: the "Metric" button.
   - On iOS: the "Use Metric Units" button.
* Slide to switch  the button of your preference.
