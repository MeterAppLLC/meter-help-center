# How to Cancel Your Meter Subscription on iOS

If you subscribed to Meter through the App Store on your iOS device and need to cancel your subscription, follow these steps:

## Step 1: Visit the Apple Support Page

1. **Go to the Official Apple Instructions**:
- Navigate to the [Apple Support Page](https://support.apple.com/118428).

## Step 2: Follow Apple’s Official Instructions

1. **Read the Instructions**:
- Follow the official Apple instructions provided on the page to **Cancel Subscriptions** on your iOS device.

> **Note:** Cancelling your subscription will take effect at the end of your current billing period as per Apple’s policies. Ensure you follow the steps accurately to manage your subscription.

If you have any questions or encounter issues, please contact our [support team](mailto:support@meterapp.co).
