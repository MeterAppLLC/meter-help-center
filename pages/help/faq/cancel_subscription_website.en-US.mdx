# ​​How do I cancel my subscription through the website?

 In order to cancel your Meter App subscription, follow these steps.

* Log in to your Meter account through the website.
* [Click here to log in](https://www.meterapp.co/ar/signin).
* Once inside your account profile, Tap on "Trips", "Pricing".
* Select Meter Pro, Then tap “Open customer portal”.
* Select the option Cancel plan and confirm.
