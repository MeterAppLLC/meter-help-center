How do I switch my email address?

Follow these steps:

Log in to your Meter account through the website.
Click here to log in.
Once inside your account profile, scroll down the page until you find the option "update email address".
Tap on the option "update email address", add your new email address, then tap "Submit email". We will send an email with a link that you must click to confirm the email change.
After you click to confirm the email change, your account will be updated with the new email address.
