# Custom Fares
Take full control of your fare structures with the ability to create and manage an unlimited number of customized fares tailored to different service levels.

**How to customize your fares?**

Follow these steps:

* Tap "Rides" and select "Custom."
* Click on "Manage Custom Fare."
* Press the + sign in the top right corner.
* Assign a name to your custom Fare.
* Add the details of your Fare.
* If you are a taxi driver, include Fares corresponding to your country and city. If you are a private driver, adjust the Fares according to your convenience.
