# How to Delete a Fleet

If you need to delete a fleet from your account, follow these simple steps:

1. **Visit the Fleet Management Page**:
- Navigate to [Manage Fleet](https://www.meterapp.co/en/fleet/manage_fleet).

2. **Locate the Delete Button**:
- Scroll to the bottom of the page.
- Click on the **Delete Fleet** button.

3. **Confirm the Deletion**:
- A confirmation prompt will appear.
- Confirm the deletion to permanently remove the fleet from your account.

> **Note:** Deleting a fleet is irreversible. Make sure to review any important data or settings before proceeding.

If you have any questions or encounter issues, please contact our [support team](mailto:support@meterapp.co).
