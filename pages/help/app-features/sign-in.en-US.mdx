# How to Open a Meter Account for Taxi Drivers: Step-by-Step Guide

## How to create a Meter account for Taxis?
To create a Meter account, here are the steps you should follow:

1. Download the Meter for Taxis application from the Play Store or the App Store.
- [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/meter-for-taxis/id931234419)
- [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=osacky.ridemeter)

2. Open the app and press the "Settings" button.
<img height="150" src="https://www.meterapp.co/IMG_8965.PNG" width="250"/>

3. Select "log in", now enter your email and select "OK".
<img height="150" src="https://www.meterapp.co/IMG_8966.PNG" width="250"/>

4. You will receive an email with a code to log in - enter the code and select "OK".
<img height="150" src="https://www.meterapp.co/IMG_8967.PNG" width="250"/>

5. Personalize your profile: First and last names or business name.
<img height="150" src="https://www.meterapp.co/IMG_8968.PNG" width="250"/>

**Sign in with Google:**
* If you're using an Android device, you can sign in to MeterApp with your Google account.
* Click on "Sign in".
* Select "Sign in with Google".
* Choose the Gmail account you want to sign in with.
* Click on "Continue".

**Sign in with Apple:**
* If you're using an Apple device, you can sign in to MeterApp with your Apple account.
* Click on "Sign in with Apple".
* Click on "Continue", then enter your password or use facial recognition to grant permission.

Done! Once you have completed the steps.
