# Dispatch a driver

* You can now dispatch drivers in your fleet

* Use the android app to dispatch a driver to a pick up location in realtime. The driver will receive a notification and will be able to navigate to the pick up location.

## Navigation Options
Choose your favorite navigation option. Google Maps, Waze, or Meter (powered by Google Maps). We recommend using Meter to navigate for the best experience!
